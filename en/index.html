AboutLearn more about my education, international experience, and work in research, teaching, and editorial practice.
Styles SassBorn in North Dakota, USA, I have gained extensive international experience throughout my academic and professional career. After completing a Bachelor's degree in Literature and Film at the University of Iowa, I spent a semester abroad at the University of the Basque Country in San Sebastin/Donostia, Spain, where I also worked as an English instructor.I continued my academic training at Heidelberg University in Germany, earning both a Master's degree and a PhD in American Studies. My doctoral research focused on campaign narratives in the context of the 2008 U.S. presidential election. I'm currently Director of the Writing Workshop at the Heidelberg Center for American Studies and serve as Assistant Director of the American Junior Year study abroad program at Heidelberg.In addition, I work as a lecturer at AKAD University and as an editor, translator, and writer. My professional focus lies in academic and editorial writing, intercultural communication, and the development and coordination of international education programs. I am currently based in Stuttgart, Germany.
